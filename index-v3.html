Boundaries

Boundaries Are Energy Infrastructure

Without them, you leak energy constantly — to other people's urgency, expectations, and emotions.

⏱ Time

When you're available, when you're not. How much time goes to the search vs. yourself.

📱 Communication

Who you respond to and how quickly. Draining texts, unsolicited advice.

💛 Emotional

How much of others' emotional states you absorb. Whose anxiety you carry.

💰 Financial

What you will and won't accept. How you communicate these limits clearly.

Boundaries aren't walls. They're decisions about how you spend your finite energy.

Boundary Experiment

Describe Your Experiment

💡 Specificity is what makes it hold

"I'll be better with time" isn't a boundary. "I won't answer career calls on Sundays" is. Vague commitments fall apart under pressure.

What exactly will you do or not do? When? With whom?

What specifically will you stop, start, or limit? What will you say if someone pushes back? What signal will tell you the boundary is working?
Key Insight

Energy Is Information

Not a Weakness Signal

When you're running low, your body isn't failing you. It's giving you data: this is unsustainable. Something needs to change.

People who build careers that last learn to listen to that data rather than override it.

This is not soft advice. This is strategy. Regulated people make better decisions, build better relationships, and interview better. Your energy management is your competitive advantage.
